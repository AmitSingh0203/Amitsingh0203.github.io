"Most RAFM professionals have seen the problem from one side. I have led it from all three — operator, vendor, and national regulator."

My career spans 17 years and three distinct vantage points: building RAFM functions inside a Tier-1 operator (Vodafone India), architecting and delivering platforms as a global vendor (Mobileum), and now leading national-level compliance and fraud programs as a Senior RAFM Specialist for Burundi's national telecom regulator.

Each perspective changed how I see the problem. Together, they make me unusually effective — I understand what operators hide, what vendors over-promise, and what regulators actually need.

My programs have spanned GSM voice, roaming, VAS, interconnect, mobile money, AML/KYC, and digital payment fraud — covering the full revenue and fraud surface of modern telecom and fintech ecosystems across Africa, the Middle East, South Asia, and Southeast Asia.
